About Us

Aroma Health and Fitness & Universal Nutrition Store

Aroma Health & Fitness Centre, proudly located in the heart of Puducherry on Nehru Street, is Pondicherry's pioneering fitness destination — the Fitness Leader in Pondicherry since 1995. With a stellar 4.8-star rating and a passionate community of members, we offer world-class training through a certified, scientific approach and method in a welcoming, family-like environment. Our expert trainers bring unmatched knowledge, dedication, and personal care to every session.

Whether your goal is weight loss, yoga, cardio or strength training, our fully equipped unisex gym caters to all fitness levels — ladies and gents. Our Assured Weight Loss Program follows a proven scientific method with measurable results. Personal Training (PT) sessions are available one-on-one, and we offer special training for celebrities requiring focused, private coaching.
