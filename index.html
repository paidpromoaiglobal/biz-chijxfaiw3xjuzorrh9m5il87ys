Aroma Health & Fitness Centre, proudly located in the heart of Puducherry on Nehru Street, is the city's pioneering fitness destination — the first gym ever established in the coastal region of Pondicherry. With a stellar 4.8-star rating and a passionate community of members, we offer world-class training in a welcoming, family-like environment. Members have achieved remarkable results — including 10 kg reductions in just two months — under our structured, personalised guidance. Open every day from early morning to evening, we make it easy for you to stay consistent and reach your fitness goals. At Aroma, we don't just train bodies — we build confidence, discipline, and a healthier lifestyle.
